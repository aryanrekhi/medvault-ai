The Problem

Indian radiology is stretched to breaking point.

Radiologist shortages, rising scan volumes, and zero audit infrastructure — while most AI solutions want your patient data on their cloud.

01

Radiologist Shortage

India has ~12,000 radiologists for 1.4 billion people. That's 100+ studies per radiologist daily — fatigue-driven errors are inevitable.

02

No Audit Trail

Most hospitals have zero tamper-proof record of who viewed a study, when it was reported, or if critical findings were acted on in time.

03

Data Sovereignty Risk

Cloud-based AI means patient DICOM images leave your hospital network. That's a compliance nightmare and a trust destroyer.
